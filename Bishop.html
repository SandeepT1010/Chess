How the bishop moves

The bishop can only move diagonally.

A light squared bishop can only move in the light squares, and the dark sqyared bishop can only move in the dark squares.

The bishop can move any number of squares as long as there are no pieces in its way.

The bishop is a minor piece.

Each player has two bishops on different colors.

The bishop is worth 3 points.
